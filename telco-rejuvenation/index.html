Summary

Together with Indonesia’s biggest telco, we translated a static, offline brand into new digital experiences to address the needs of 163 million customers across the country.
Our work included research in and around Jakarta, a content strategy, a new visual design direction and interaction models for both their app and website.
We documented the design in a digital design language system (DLS) with components and templates in code, allowing Telkomsel to create consistent and delightful experiences at scale. The work went live on June 18 2021.
Design challenge (*mobile app design squad limited)

“How might we improve the overall app performance without missing essential information for customers while satisfying the company’s business initiative?”
