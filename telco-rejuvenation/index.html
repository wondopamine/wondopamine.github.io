TL;DR

Together with Indonesia’s biggest telco, we translated a static, offline brand into new digital experiences to address the needs of 163 million customers across the country.
Our work included research in and around Jakarta, a content strategy, a new visual design direction and interaction models for both their app and website.
We documented the design in a digital design language system (DLS) with components and templates in code, allowing Telkomsel to create consistent and delightful experiences at scale. And the work went live on June 18 2021. LINK

Design challenge

“How might we provide a seamless, simple, and reliable brand experience for its customers, together with personalization that brings more relevant and delightful experiences to build stronger relationships by empowering customers to do more with Telkomsel?”

Interaction Design Strategy

01 Interaction Principles

Interaction design principles are fundamental to establish how Telkomsel should deliver excellent experiences to customers while ensuring that business goals are considered.
Decisions made in the future should uphold the integrity of these values: Contextual, Curated, and Rewarding.

02 User Intents

Understanding the modes behind different journeys as a starting point for designing interactions that are tailored to users’ expectations and mental models.

03 Information architecture

The information architecture describes, in the form of a tree-structure, how information and content across the MyTelkomsel app pages are located in relationship to each other.

04 Interaction Model

Interaction models define the structure and behaviour of interactive systems. This gives us a common vision of how users move from one place to another within Telkomsel.com and MyTelkomsel App, aligned across functional and business teams.
