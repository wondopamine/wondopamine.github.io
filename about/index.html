Wondo Jeong is a freelance UX designer specialized in digital and tangible interface design.
Most recently, he graduated Design Practice and Research Lab at UNIST, specializing in interaction design and engineering to seek the most proper design questions to vivid design solutions.
He previously delivered works ranged from mobile app to IoT products for companies from various industries such as IKEA, Hyundai Motor Company, LG, L’Oreal and Azimut Yacht as an interaction designer in Italy and South Korea.
Recently, he has been nominated and achieved design awards across A’, iF and red-dot design award and has shown his academic works at DIS (ACM SIGCHI) 2019 the international HCI conference, IASDR 2019 the design research conference and HCI 2020. He exhibited his design works at Design Korea, Seoul Design Festival, KSDS international invitational design exhibition, and shown his interactive art installation in International Arts Education Week. He has run the alternative design and art gallery in his hometown, Ulsan at an earlier time.

Wonder why, zoom in and out, connect dots, design and deliver.
